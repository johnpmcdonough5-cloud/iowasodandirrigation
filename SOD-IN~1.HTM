Your first two weeks

Give Your New Lawn the Best Start

  • Water every day to keep the soil moist so roots settle in deep.
  • Give it a gentle tug — if it holds tight, the roots are working.
  • Mow at about 3 inches, keeping the blade high to avoid stress.
  • Clear away clippings so air and sunlight keep your lawn healthy.
