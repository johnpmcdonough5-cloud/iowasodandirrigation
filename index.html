Frequently Asked Questions

Should I reseed my lawn or lay fresh sod?
Sod gives instant, uniform results with minimal downtime. It's ideal for lawns that are badly damaged, weedy, or bare — a fresh start, fast.
Can you guarantee my sod will take root?
Yes. With expert installation and our Establishment Plan, your new lawn gets a solid foundation through the critical first weeks. Follow our simple care steps and you'll be on your way to a thriving yard.
How do I care for my lawn after you leave?
We don't leave you on your own. You'll get a personalized watering schedule, weekly check-ins, and guidance through the first mow and final quality check.
I'm worried my sprinklers waste water — what are my options?
Our smart irrigation systems spray only where and when needed, using up to 50% less water. You save water, lower your bills, and still get a healthy, green lawn.
Can I really get a free quote in minutes?
Absolutely. Once you reach out, one of our installers contacts you to gather details and provide a clear ballpark quote on the spot.
