Common questions

Before You Book

How much does sod installation cost?
Sod installation typically runs a few dollars per square foot, fully installed — covering old lawn removal, soil grading, premium sod, labor, rolling, starter fertilizer, and cleanup. Your phone estimate gives you a ballpark within about 5% of the final price.
How much does a smart irrigation system cost?
Pricing depends on lot size, number of zones, and layout. We'll size the system to your yard and give you a clear quote that includes design, installation, heads, controller, and setup.
How long does installation take?
Most sod installs finish in a single day, with ground prep being the bulk of the work. Your new lawn is ready for normal use within a few weeks. Irrigation also installs in about a day for most homes.
What happens after I submit this form?
You get an instant confirmation, then a call from our team — usually within a few hours. Reply with photos of your yard to help us give the most accurate quote.
Do you offer a combo discount for sod + irrigation?
Yes. Book sod and irrigation together and receive 5% off the total project cost — plus one crew, one timeline, and less disruption to your yard.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Waterloo, Cedar Falls, Cedar Rapids, Iowa City, Waverly, and nearby communities. Not sure if we cover you? Submit the form and we'll let you know right away.
